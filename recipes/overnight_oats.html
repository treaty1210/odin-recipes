Overnight Oats

Description

An easy to make dish containing oats. Feel free to modify the dish if you prefer other nuts and/or fruits. Alternatives to other dairy products can also be used.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup steel cut oats
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened shredded coconut (Can be switched for other fruits)
  • 1 tablespoon crushed pecans (Can be switched with other nuts)
  • 1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup milk (Can be switched with alternatives to dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon milk (Can be switched with alternatives to dairy)

Steps

  1. Mix oats, coconut, pecans, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small container
  2. Add 1/3 cup of milk to container.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
  4. Stir 1 tablespoon milk into mixture before serving.
