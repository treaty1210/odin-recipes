Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

A dish containing shrimp, rice, and other assorted vegetables. This is a simple recipe that anyone can cook. Just follow the recipe listed and adjust it to your specific preference. If you feel that it could use more salt, then add more salt. The process of cooking is experimental, find out the one that fits best for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked jasmine rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons, vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
  • 16 ounce package of frozen peas and carrots
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Pour 3 cups of water into a saucepan with rice and let it boil. Reduce the head to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked and water is absorbed. This should take around 20 minutes. Set aside and let it cool.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat. From there, add onions and cook until they are soft and translucent which takes around 5 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Mix in the cooked and cooled rice. Add in the frozen peas and carrot mix; fry until rice starts to brown. Add in 2 tablespoons of butter and stir. Pour in eggs and cook until firm. Add oyster and soy sauce and 1/2 lemon juice. Stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat remaining vegetable oil, 1 tablespoon in a separate pan on med-high heat. Add shrimp and fry until they are bright pink on outside and meat is opaque.
  5. Mix in remaining tablespoons of butter and lemon juice and combine with fried rice mixture.
